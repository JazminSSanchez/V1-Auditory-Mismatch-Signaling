Functions used by other programs in the creation of graphs. 
Written by Jazmin Sanchez 2025

## ExtractDataMultiChannel
Read and save in a structure the complete spikedata, spikedata9 and the hole BST spikedata9 just includes trials that have 9 standard before the deviant in the oddball paradigm, and in the case of the controls and periodic oddball it takes the first 9 sequences. 

## Mann_Kendall.m
Performs original Mann-Kendall test of the null hypothesis of trend absence in the vector V, against the alternative of trend.

## pathsMultichannel
Creates a table with all the directories of interest from a specific area.

## ReadPCAPaths.m
Reads all the folders saved in the specified path

## ReadUnitTableMultichannel.m
Reads an excel spreadsheet with the data of unit table

##shadedErrorBar.m
Generates a continuous error bar area around a line plot

## ttest_bonf.m
Performs a set of Bonferroni-corrected t-tests to determine which pairs of treatments could have the same mean.

## fdr_bh.m
Executes the Benjamini & Hochberg (1995) and the Benjamini & Yekutieli (2001) procedure for controlling the false discovery rate (FDR) of a family of hypothesis tests.
