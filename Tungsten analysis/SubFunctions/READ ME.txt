Functions used by other programs in the analysis of tungsten recording. 
Written by Jazmin Sanchez 2024

## beutify.m
Gives format to MATLAB graphs

## ExtractMatFiles.m
Read and save in a structure the complete spikedata, spikedata9 and the hole BST
spikedata9 just includes trials that have 9 standard before the deviant in the oddball paradigm, and in the case of the controls ans periodic oddball it takes the first 9 sequences.

## IndexGeneratorDensity.m 
Generating a normalized index table

## pathsPCA.m
Creates a table with all the directories of interest

## ReadPCAPaths.m
Reads all the folders saved in the specified path

## ReadUnitTable.m
Reads an excel spreadsheet with the data of the units recorded

## CSI_bootstrap2.m
Does a bootstrapping calculation of the CSI index.
